# Welcome to DungeonMaister!

This tutorial will guide you through creating your first character and learning the basics of Dungeons & Dragons.

## Step 1: Character Creation

Let's start by creating your character. A character is defined by their race, class, and ability scores.

### Choose a Race

There are many races to choose from in D&D, each with unique abilities. For this tutorial, we'll choose from a few common ones:

*   **Human:** Versatile and ambitious.
*   **Elf:** Graceful and long-lived, with a connection to magic.
*   **Dwarf:** Resilient and skilled artisans.

**DM:** "Welcome, traveler! Before we begin our adventure, we need to know who you are. Tell me, what is your race? Are you a Human, an Elf, or a Dwarf?"

### Choose a Class

Your class determines your skills and abilities. Here are a few options:

*   **Fighter:** A master of combat.
*   **Wizard:** A powerful magic-user.
*   **Rogue:** A sneaky and skillful adventurer.

**DM:** "Next, what is your class? Are you a valiant Fighter, a wise Wizard, or a cunning Rogue?"

### Determine Ability Scores

Your character has six ability scores: Strength (STR), Dexterity (DEX), Constitution (CON), Intelligence (INT), Wisdom (WIS), and Charisma (CHA). These scores determine your character's strengths and weaknesses.

We will use the standard array to set your ability scores: 15, 14, 13, 12, 10, 8. You can assign these scores to your abilities as you see fit.

**DM:** "Now, let's determine your abilities. We have the scores 15, 14, 13, 12, 10, and 8 to assign to your Strength, Dexterity, Constitution, Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma. How would you like to assign them?"

### Name Your Character

Finally, give your character a name.

**DM:** "Every hero needs a name. What is yours?"
